About Our Village

Warud is a beautiful village located in Shindkheda Taluka of Dhule district in Maharashtra. The village is known for its peaceful environment, agriculture, and cultural traditions.

The people of the village live together with unity and harmony. Agriculture is the main occupation and many festivals and religious events are celebrated with great enthusiasm.

The village also has schools, temples, and natural beauty which make it a wonderful place to live.
