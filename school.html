ZP Primary School

Our village ZP Primary School holds a special place in the hearts of villagers. Earlier, the teachers whom we respectfully called "Guruji"were known for their discipline and dedication. Because of their strict guidance, students studied with focus and sincerity. The presence of Guruji created an environment where children respected education and worked hard in their studies. Even today, the school continues to provide quality education and shape the future of village students.

JNV High School

The entrance gate of our village high school stands as a symbol of knowledge and opportunity for every student who walks through it. For many years, this gate has welcomed generations of students who came here with dreams of learning and building a better future. Every morning, students pass through this gate with enthusiasm to gain education, discipline, and values. The school campus behind this gate has witnessed countless memories of learning, friendship, and growth. It remains a proud landmark of education and inspiration for the entire village community.

The village High School plays an important role in shaping the future of many students. For generations, it has been a place where young minds receive quality education and guidance from dedicated teachers. The teachers are not only educators but also mentors who inspire students to work hard and achieve their dreams. The school provides a disciplined and supportive learning environment where students develop knowledge, confidence, and good values. Many successful individuals from the village began their educational journey in this high school, making it a proud symbol of learning and growth for the entire community.
